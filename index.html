WAVE ENERGY
Generated from the surface movement of the Ocean's waves.
SOLAR ENERGY
Harnessed from sunlight using solar panels or thermal collectors.
HYDRO ENERGY
Produced from the flow of water, typically using dams to generate eletricity
TIDAL ENERGY
Captured from rise and fall of tides,using underwater turbines and other technologies
GEOTHERMAL ENERGY
Derived from the heat stored beneath the Earth's surface ,used for heating and eletricity generation.
WIND ENERGY
Generated by wind turbines that convert wind movements into eletricity
